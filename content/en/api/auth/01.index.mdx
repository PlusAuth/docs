<h1 class="mt-0">PlusAuth Protocol Specifications Reference</h1>

This document is contains Authorization and Authentication related protocol endpoints and their usages.

::alert{type=info}
PlusAuth is a [certified](https://openid.net/developers/certified-openid-connect-implementations/) OpenID Connect Provider. For OpenId Connect related endpoints/usages you can also follow
[OpenID Connect Specification](https://openid.net/specs/openid-connect-core-1_0.html)
<br>
Your tenant's OpenID Connect metadata is accessible from
[https://YOUR_TENANT_ID.plusauth.com/.well-known/openid-configuration](https://YOUR_TENANT_ID.plusauth.com/.well-known/openid-configuration).
You can find related endpoints and some additional helpers from the metadata json.

::
